Welcome to Historic Lowertown

A gagillion artists live/work here!

Actually, Lowertown is home to several hundred artists, and the oh-so popular Spring/Fall Art Crawls.

The neighborhood's buildings (and the neighborhood itself) are registered in the Natioinal Historic Registry. Architecture boasts a great mixture of historic and modern design. It's just cool. Now if only we had a grocery store, everything would be perfect.
